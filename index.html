Learn about your medications

It is important to take your medications exactly as prescribed. Do not skip doses or stop taking them without talking to your doctor first. Some conditions may get worse if the medication is stopped suddenly. Make sure you know how to store your medications and keep them out of the reach of children. Keep a list of all your medications with you, and share the list with your doctor and pharmacist.
